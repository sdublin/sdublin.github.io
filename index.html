Private lessons via Skype

In my five years as a language instructor, I’ve seen many students succeed in learning German. What did they all have in common? Each made the fastest improvement with personal instruction in a one-on-one setting. My private German lessons are customized to your goals, intensive, and flexible to fit your schedule.

Learn from the comfort of your home

Learning German online is a convenient way to receive lessons from a native speaker from your home, on your own schedule. Live online lessons run exactly like in-person lessons. Same content, same interactions. The only difference is that we communicate live via the internet.

Personalized lessons

Why do people want to learn German? The reasons vary from person to person, so you can set the goals. Do you want to talk to German customers or are you preparing for a trip to a german-speaking country? Private lessons have the major advantage that you get a lot of speaking time and therefore quickly build up your confidence to talk in real-life situations.

About Me

My name is Dr. Marike Herold-Dublin. I’ve been teaching German as a foreign language professionally for five years. I’m trained and certified by the Goethe-Institute and teach according to their communicative approach method. I attended university and worked in the US for over ten years, so I understand the challenges of learning a language and trying to thrive in a foreign country. Through my personal experiences, I developed several successful strategies that I'll pass on to you.
