About Me

My name is Marike Herold-Dublin. I’ve been teaching German as a foreign language professionally for five years. I’m trained and certified by the Goethe-Institute and teach according to their communicative approach method. I attended university and worked in the US for over ten years, so I understand the challenges my students face learn a foreign language and trying to thrive in a foreign country. Through these experiences, I developed several successful strategies that I want to pass on to you. Being married to an American living in Germany, I accompany him daily in the integration process here in Germany, experiencing first-hand the challenges of learning the German language and culture. This helps me see the language from the point of view of the student.
