What do I need for an online lesson?

You will need a stable internet connection and Skype (or a similar program) installed on your computer. A computer headset is optional, but recommended for the best learning experience.

Do you offer a trial lesson?

Yes. I offer a free trial lesson. This way we can see if it's a good match with respect to teaching style and goals.

How long are your lessons?

Lessons are 60 minutes.

How do I pay for my lessons?

Paypal or direct bank transfer are the two prefered payment methods.

Can I reschedule my lesson?

Yes. If you can't make a scheduled lesson, just contact me and we will arrange for a new time based on the available slots.

What is your cancellation policy?

A full refund will be made for all lessons cancelled by the language teacher. If you are unable to attend a scheduled lesson, you can reschedule based on the available time slots. A refund is generally not made for lessons cancelled by the student.
